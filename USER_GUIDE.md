# POS Mocha User Guide

## Welcome to POS Mocha

POS Mocha is your complete restaurant and hotel management system. Whether you're serving guests in the dining room, managing hotel rooms, or running the kitchen, this guide will help you use all the features effectively.

## üîê Getting Started - Login & Navigation

### How to Log In
1. Open POS Mocha on your device
2. Enter your **Employee ID** and **PIN**
3. The system will automatically show your available features based on your role

### Understanding Your Dashboard
- **Top Bar**: Order type buttons (Dine In, Takeaway, Delivery, Room Service)
- **Icon Bar**: Quick access to different sections (Menu, Bar Sales, Rooms, Tables, Dashboard)
- **Main Area**: Current view (menu, orders, reports, etc.)
- **Right Panel**: Current order details and checkout

## üçΩÔ∏è Restaurant Operations

### Taking Orders

#### For Dine-In Customers
1. Click **"Dine In"** in the top bar
2. Select a **table** from the table layout (if tables are managed)
3. Browse the **menu** and tap items to add them to the order
4. Use the **+** and **-** buttons to adjust quantities
5. Add **special notes** for any customizations
6. Click **"Add to Order"** or tap items directly

#### For Takeaway Orders
1. Click **"Takeaway"** in the top bar
2. Add items to the order as usual
3. Enter customer **name** and **phone number**
4. Process payment when ready for pickup

#### For Delivery Orders
1. Click **"Delivery"** in the top bar
2. Add items to the order
3. Enter complete **delivery address** and **customer details**
4. Add any **delivery instructions**
5. The system will assign a driver automatically

#### For Room Service
1. Click **"Room Service"** in the top bar
2. Select the **guest's room number**
3. Add items to the order
4. Orders go directly to the kitchen and guest's bill

### Managing Your Order
- **View Order**: Check the right panel to see current items
- **Modify Items**: Tap any item to change quantity or add notes
- **Remove Items**: Use the **trash icon** next to items
- **Split Payments**: Use the split payment option for groups
- **Apply Discounts**: Add percentage or fixed amount discounts

### Processing Payments
1. Review the order total in the right panel
2. Select payment method:
   - **Cash**: Enter amount received
   - **Card**: Process card payment
   - **Mobile Money**: Enter transaction details
   - **Room Charge**: For hotel guests
3. Print or email receipt
4. Complete the order

## üè® Hotel Operations

### Managing Rooms (Receptionists)

#### Check-In Process
1. Click the **Building icon** (Rooms) in the top bar
2. Find the vacant room
3. Click **"Check In"**
4. Enter guest details:
   - Full name
   - Phone number
   - Check-in/check-out dates
   - Payment method
5. Assign room rate
6. Process payment if required

#### Check-Out Process
1. Find the occupied room
2. Click **"Check Out"**
3. Review any charges (room service, minibar, etc.)
4. Process final payment
5. Print receipt

#### Room Status Updates
- **Vacant**: Room is ready for new guests
- **Occupied**: Guest is staying
- **Reserved**: Booked for future arrival
- **Maintenance**: Under repair
- **Cleaning**: Housekeeping in progress

### Housekeeping Tasks

#### Daily Room Cleaning
1. Log in with your housekeeping account
2. View rooms that need cleaning
3. Update room status:
   - **Cleaning**: When you start
   - **Vacant**: When finished
4. Submit maintenance requests for any issues found

#### Maintenance Requests
1. Click on a room needing repairs
2. Select **"Report Issue"**
3. Choose priority: Low, Medium, High, Urgent
4. Describe the problem
5. Submit for management review
